Detailed Description

A simplified priority queue implementation. Is stable, auto-resizes, and thread-safe, at the cost of being slightly slower than FastPriorityQueue Methods tagged as O(1) or O(log n) are assuming there are no duplicates. Duplicates may increase the algorithmic complexity.

A simplified priority queue implementation. Is stable, auto-resizes, and thread-safe, at the cost of being slightly slower than FastPriorityQueue This class is kept here for backwards compatibility. It's recommended you use SimplePriorityQueue<TItem, TPriority>.
